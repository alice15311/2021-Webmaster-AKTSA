What's New

Allison Vicera, 1/7/21

2021 is a new year and the team at KatKure is eager to jump right in! Read below about how we are making strides to expand and provide more support for you this coming year.

With the coming new year, the team here at KatKure has decided to ramp up their goals! We have lots of new features and processes to help make you and your journey a better one. Below is a list of recently released and upcoming projects or fixes to certain aspects of the company. Further details will come out later.

Stock and Orders

We have recently redone our supply chain! With our new partners for this year we can get you your medicine faster than before. Better restocking and prices to come.

Previously, many of the products were made to order and supplied slowly due to this. For the first time since our conception, we have created a more affordable and supportable supply system to help supply the many more customers we have received.

Although we have changed the supply chain, there will still be a lack of stock due to lower manufacturing and availability during these times. The pandemic has hit our suppliers hard and we will continue to experience a shortage. We appreciate your support and understanding during these hard times. For all those in a subscription based plan, this should not impact you and your supply.
