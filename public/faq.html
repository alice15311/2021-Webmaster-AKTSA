FAQs

What can I do with KatKure?

We provide two main services: virtual therapy sessions, and an online shop where you can buy medications without any addictive substances in them whatsoever.

You can find more details about both on this FAQ page, and also by checking out our pages about our services here and exploring the products we offer in our shop here.

How can I get started on here?

If you want to participate in therapy sessions, start by creating an account on our platform, and answer a few questions so we can get to know you better. You'll be able to choose a counselor and find group therapy sessions in your account dashboard.

For the shop, no account is needed! Simply browse our products for medication that you need - such as those that relieve headaches - and add them to your cart. You'll be able to upload your prescriptions on paper or use your doctor's e-prescription in order to verify your purchases, and then checkout and wait for delivery!

Even if I am not a former drug addict, can I still use KatKure?

Of course! We provide for anyone who is wary of the addictive qualities of opioids and want a safe, effective alternative.

Our therapy sessions are also open to all who want to join. Although many group sessions are tailored towards rehabilitation of former drug addicts, you can find other groups with different focuses on our diverse platform, as well.

Questions about Therapy

Have questions about the services offered on our platform? Don't worry, we've answered some of the most common that crop up below, from pricing to group therapy!

How does the therapy work?

KatKure offers individual as well as group sessions along with the support of highly trained professionals who have many years of experience under their belt, and are very skilled at what they do. All of the sessions will be virtual and a platform of communication will be provided between you are your therapist.

We can assure you that your experience here at KatKure will be nothing less of fantastic!

How is pricing determined?

Pricing varies depending on your counselor and what you need. No person is ever the same, and we understand that - our plans are meant to be as flexible as possible while giving you the care you need.

Baseline prices are listed under our pricing plans, and in the majority of cases will not be subject to change.

Additional fees may be added on depending on the level of availability your counselor will expect to maintain and the number of meetings you feel you need during a month. Extra meetings not included in the general plan may also add to the total depending on your counselor.

How do I find a counselor?

First, create an account here on our platform and log in. You should be prompted to answer a few quick questions about you in order to let us know you better.

After you've finished those questions, we will process your request and try and find a match for you based on your responses. You'll be able to see which counselors we've recommended for you in your account dashboard here on KatKure after a few hours.

You can then review the counselor matches, see their profiles, and send requests to ones you would like to meet before choosing which counselor you will be with. If you already had a counselor in mind, you can search them up in your dashboard and also send them a request.

If you are not seeing any results or are getting an error message, please contact help@katkure.com so we can help you figure out the problem.

Is there group therapy?

Yes, we have an option for group therapy available on our platform!

You can search through groups in your dashboard once you've signed up for a KatKure account. You can find groups with filters like with number of people, whether it's open or closed, or their focus. Once you've found one you like, you can send a request to join and to try out the group.

How often do I meet with my counselor?

This is completely up to you here at KatKure - schedule your meetings around your needs and around your schedule.

At least one live session a month is included as part of our base plan, and you can schedule more each month if you feel the need, or you can stick with communicating through text with your counselor between meetings. Length of the meetings is also variable, but you are entitled to at least 60 minutes per month with the baseline pay for each plan.

Questions about Our Shop

Have questions about prescriptions, delivery, or return policy? Find everything you need to know about the common questions that we've been asked about our shop over the years!

Are these medications effective?

Yes, all medications sold in our shop have been proven to be very effective in what they do! We have looked at various medical studies in order to make 100% sure that what we're selling in our store will truly help you.

We're proud to say that without opioids or other addictive substances, these medications will still provide powerful relief and fantastic services, without the risk of relapsing!

How long does delivery take?

Delivery will depend on the location you are in. We provide services to all 50 states plus Puerto Rico, and if you live within these regions, you should be able to get your order within 4 days.

If more than 4 days have passed and you have not yet received your order, please contact us at help@katkure.com for customer support.

What is your return policy?

Unopened, new medications will be able to be returned to us up to 30 days from the original purchase. We will not reuse this medication or resell it as part of stock due to safety guidelines put forth by the federal government.

Please mail all returning packages to 1111 Alberry Road, Charlotte, NC, 1111.

How do I send my verification for my patient’s prescription?

You can send it electronically to verification@katkure.com or by fax to 011-222-1113. You will get a confirmation email if the submission has been properly processed.

If you are still not able to get a verification for the prescription after 5 business days, please contact help@katkure.com about this problem. We will be happy to assist you!

My medication isn’t there, where do I get it?

Sometimes the availability of medication may depend on the region of delivery and the time frame with which you need it delivered. Please email help@katkure.com for more information or to request that a medication be added, and we will review your request promptly.

How do I change my shipping address?

We do not store your address - when you enter in your details, you may put your new address there!
