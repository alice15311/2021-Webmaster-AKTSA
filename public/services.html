Our Platform

With KatKure, you can access quality care anywhere and anytime you need it. Our platform is built to be intuitive, user-friendly, and convenient - no more long waiting times, or driving to and from meetings, or worrying about the hassle of rescheduling! Even the largest of distances can be closed with our comprehensive program.

Take advantage of the variety of services we can offer, from text, video, and audio exchanges with counselors and live sessions, to group sessions and other live events!

Group sessions

Some of the most powerful and effective therapy strategies are group counseling sessions. It may sound scary to talk with strangers at first, but many psychologists say that people are surprised at how rewarding they can be!

Talking and listening to others discussing their own struggles can help you figure out strategies that you can apply to your own life. You can get ideas and advice from other members, realize that you're not alone, and develop a lifelong support network along the way.

On our platform, you can find a wide array of groups ranging from 5 to 15 people in size, all led by one of our trusted professional counselors. You can easily match with potential groups with our convenient search function that filters based on criteria like when and how long they meet for, as well as group size.

But if group counseling isn't for you, that's okay! With KatKure, you can choose to attend only individual counseling, or only group sessions, or both. We've always believed that we should be here to support you however we can.

Pricing

At KatKure, we're committed to providing excellent, quality care at an affordable price. Access to care shouldn't be limited by financial means, and so with that in mind, our pricing for our plans is meant to be the most affordable for everyone. Exact pricing may vary on your specific needs, like length and amount of sessions.

Includes one live session and the ability to message with text, audio, and video with a counselor for a week.

You can use this to test out the platform and see if it works for you before deciding.

Price range: free

Individual Sessions

Group Sessions
