About Us

It's why we're dedicated to creating alternative medicines that doesn't contain addictive substances and drugs. With the current pandemic, many former addicts are refusing treatments for their ongoing illnesses due to the fear of relapsing. In fact, according to the PMC, physicians face tough choices in prescribing pain medications for both chronic and acute illnesses for the 5%-17% of afflicted individuals.
