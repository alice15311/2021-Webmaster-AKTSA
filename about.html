About Us

We believe that everybody deserves a second chance to turn their life around. Our company offers former drug addicts a way to receive alternative medications for their ailments in order to redirect the prescripton misuse cycle and permanently keep former drug addicts from using again.

It's why we're dedicated to creating alternative medicines that doesn't contain addictive substances and drugs. With the current pandemic, many former addicts are refusing treatments for their ongoing illnesses due to the fear of relapsing. In addition, many physicians are faced with tough decisions in trying to prescribe pain medications due to the inadequate pain relief caused by the limited opiod exposure as well as the fear of indirectly causing a new relapse.

With the complete removal of opiods and addictive substances from our medication, we are offering a life-changing and efficient alternative to both addicts and their physicians. With this in mind, we mandate that each purchase from our company shall be accompanied by a physician's note.
